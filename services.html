Services

Our Services

Tailored solutions for your personal and business's financial success.

Accounting

We provide professional accounting and bookkeeping services for businesses of all sizes and industries. We handle your financial records, transactions, reports, and statements, ensuring accuracy and compliance

Company Registration

We handle all processes, including paperwork filing, legal compliance, and name reservation. Our goal is to make starting your business as hassle-free as possible, letting you focus on your entrepreneurial journey.

Tax Advisory

We provide comprehensive tax guidance, ensuring you navigate the complexities of tax laws confidently. We offer personalized strategies to minimize your tax liability, optimizing your financial well-being

Tax Clearance & Statutory Returns

We help you file your tax returns accurately and on time, ensuring compliance with authorities. We also assist you in obtaining tax clearance certificates, which are essential for various business transactions.

Business Planning

We help you create a clear and realistic business plan, covering market analysis, financial projections, and risk assessment. We also provide ongoing support to help you achieve your goals and grow your business.

PRAZ Registration

Registering your business with PRAZ is mandatory for all suppliers of goods and services to the public sector. We handle all processes, including renewals, ensuring you comply with procurement laws and regulations.

Vendor Number Registration

We assist you in obtaining a vendor number, a unique identifier for your business allowing you to participate in public tenders and contracts. We handle all processes, saving you time and hassle.

Payroll

We manage your payroll function, ensuring that your employees are paid accurately and on time. We also handle tax deductions and benefits. Our payroll service is secure, reliable, and cost-effective.

Personal & Corporate Financial Management

Our financial solutions help you optimize your income, expenses, assets, and liabilities. We provide financial planning, budgeting, and reporting services tailored to your specific needs and objectives.

Corporate Governance Issues

We advise you on best practices and standards, which are essential for accountability, transparency, and sustainability. We also help you monitor and evaluate your governance performance and outcomes.

Start-Up & SMEs Business Advisory

We offer strategic guidance, and training services, covering areas such as business development, marketing, innovation, and funding, helping you to overcome your challenges and seize opportunities.

Accounting Systems Installation & Training

We can set up the best accounting system for your business, streamlining your financial operations. We also provide training, ensuring your staff are proficient and confident in using the system.

Microsoft Excel Training

We teach you how to use the powerful features of Excel, such as formulas, charts, and more. We also provide practical exercises and tips, helping you master Excel and enhance your productivity.
